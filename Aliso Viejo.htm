Project Details

The homeowner sought to modernize their residence with smart technology, focusing on convenience, security, and energy efficiency. They wanted all smart devices to work seamlessly together and be easily controlled via a centralized system.

Scope of the Project

  • Smart Devices Installed:
    • Smart Lighting System: Installed dimmable and color-adjustable lights throughout the house, enabling customized ambiance and energy savings.
    • Smart Locks: Provided secure, keyless entry for all exterior doors, with remote locking and unlocking capabilities.
    • Smart Cameras: Set up indoor and outdoor cameras with real-time monitoring, motion detection, and cloud storage for video footage.
    • Smart Thermostats: Optimized climate control for different zones of the house, ensuring comfort and energy efficiency.
    • Leak Detectors: Installed sensors to detect water leaks and alert the homeowner before damage could occur.
    • LSmoke and CO Detectors: Enhanced safety with connected alarms that send real-time notifications to the homeowner.
    • Smart Plugs and Outlets: Automated everyday appliances like coffee makers and entertainment systems.
  • Centralized Control Panel:
    • SInstalled an intuitive control panel in a central location within the home, providing easy access to all smart devices.
    • Designed custom scenes such as "Good Morning," "Movie Night," and "Away Mode" to simplify everyday routines.
  • Mobile App Integration:
    • SSet up a mobile app for remote control and real-time alerts, ensuring the homeowner could manage their devices from anywhere.
    • Enabled tracking and management of energy usage to optimize utility costs.
